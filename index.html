About Us

Our Mission

BlueCarbonChain is dedicated to revolutionizing blue carbon ecosystem restoration through blockchain technology. Our mission is to provide a transparent, verifiable and efficient system for monitoring, reporting and verification (MRV) of carbon sequestration projects.

Why Blue Carbon?

Blue carbon ecosystems (mangroves, seagrasses, and salt marshes) are among the most efficient carbon sinks on Earth. However, they face significant threats from coastal development and climate change. Our platform enables effective restoration and conservation of these vital ecosystems.

Our Technology

Using blockchain technology, we create an immutable record of restoration activities and carbon credit generation. This ensures transparency, prevents double-counting, and builds trust among all stakeholders including NGOs, communities, investors, and regulators.

Introduction to BlueCarbonChain

BlueCarbonChain is a blockchain-powered platform designed to bring transparency and efficiency to blue carbon ecosystem restoration projects. Our system provides an end-to-end solution for Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification (MRV) of carbon sequestration activities.

This documentation will help you understand how to use our platform effectively, from data upload to carbon credit trading.

Getting Started

To begin using BlueCarbonChain, follow these steps:

  1. Register your organization using the registration form
  2. Connect your cryptocurrency wallet to the platform
  3. Set up your first project in the Projects section
  4. Begin uploading monitoring data through the Data Upload interface
  5. Once verified, your carbon credits will be available in the Marketplace

Data Upload Guide

Proper data collection and upload is essential for carbon credit verification. We accept the following data types:

  • Satellite Imagery: High-resolution satellite images of project areas
  • Drone Surveys: Orthomosaic maps and 3D models from drone flights
  • Field Measurements: Ground-truthing data including biomass samples
  • Sensor Data: Continuous monitoring from IoT sensors

All data should be uploaded in standardized formats with complete metadata. Our system will automatically verify data integrity and consistency before processing.
